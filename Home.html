About Us

Welcome to Cuts Funked Lawn Care, where every blade counts! I'm Aleesha, the owner and operator of this lawn mowing business. With a passion for precision and a love for well-groomed lawns, I've been dedicated to providing top-notch lawn care services to the Toowoomba community.

As a one-person operation, I take pride in personally tending to each lawn with care and attention to detail. Whether it's regular lawn mowing, edging, hedge trimming, or garden cleanups, I'm here to transform your outdoor space into a pristine paradise.

At Cuts Funked, customer satisfaction is my priority. I understand the value of a beautifully maintained lawn, and I'm committed to delivering the best results every time. Contact me today for a quote, and let's make your lawn the envy of the neighborhood!
